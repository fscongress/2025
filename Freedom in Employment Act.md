# AN ACT

to allow Freedom in Employment

*Preamble: In conjunction with surveying and talking with department heads from the Firestone Department of Public Works, the Firestone Department of Corrections, and the Firestone Aviation Administration- these three members of the house have determined an urgent and growing concern to be addressed by the legislature. The main purposes of this act are to allow for individuals to have active employment within 3 secondaries and have the Firestone Aviation Administration become a voluntary department.*

</div>

---

*Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the State of Firestone in Congress assembled,*

**SECTION I: TITLE**

a) This act shall be known as the “Freedom in Employment Act”, shortened to “FEA”.

**SECTION II: ENACTMENT**
a) This Bill shall go into effect as soon as the required constitutional processes have been met.

**SECTION III: ENFORCEMENT**
a) This amendment shall be enforced by any relevant parties.

**SECTION IV: DEFINITIONS**
a) The Employment Act of 2023 should be defined as [such](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1FFQ_aZH_pQZfQrcq-CmdQSzH6WeuOzGw/view).

**SECTION V: SEVERIBILITY**
(a) Should any part of this Act be struck down and/or declared unenforceable, then that part
shall be severed with the rest remaining in full force and effect unless all is struck down
and/or declared unenforceable.

**SECTION VI: AMENDMENTS**
a) Section 11 of the Employment Act of 2023 (“Secondary Departments”) is amended by creating subsection (c) which shall read “The limitation of a persons employment in secondary departments defined in subsection (a) shall be set to three.”
b) Section 11 of the Employment Act of 2023 (“Secondary Departments”) is amended by removing subsection (a)(vi) (“Firestone Aviation Administration or Firestone Department of Aviation”). Nothing shall be renumbered to fill the gap created.
c) Section 12 of the Employment Act of 2023 (“Voluntary Departments”) is amended by creating subsection (a)(vii) which shall read “[Firestone Aviation Administration;](https://www.roblox.com/communities/15301612/Firestone-Aviation-Administration#!/about)”; and inserting the word “and” at the end of subsection (a)(vi) (“Firestone Department of Commerce”).
