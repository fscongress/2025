&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; *Be it enacted by the State of Firestone Congress here assembled,*

**§ 1. TITLE**

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (a) This Act may be known as "An Act to Clarify Municipal Jurisdictions”.

**§ 2. ENACTMENT INTO LAW**

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (a) This Act shall go into effect upon the completion of the constitutionally required processes.

**§ 3. SEVERABILITY**

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (a) Should any part of this Legislation be struck down and/or declared unenforceable, then that part shall be severed with the rest remaining in full force and effect unless all is struck down and/or declared unenforceable.

**§ 4. AMENDMENTS**

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (a) Section 11(b) of the Inferior Governments Act shall be amended by striking the words “the areas defined in subsections (e), (f), and (g) of SECTION 10 of this Act.”, and inserting in their place the words “the following areas;”.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (b) Section 11(b)(i) of the Inferior Governments Act shall be created and shall state "(i) The municipal police force of the City of Redwood shall have physical jurisdiction over all places highlighted red in [this](https://gyazo.com/4f7c8351f50f20cc6af2aa26b3e784a6) image.".

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (c) Section 11(b)(ii) of the Inferior Governments Act shall be created and shall state "(ii) The municipal police force of the City of Arborfield shall have physical jurisdiction over all places highlighted in blue in [this](https://gyazo.com/4f7c8351f50f20cc6af2aa26b3e784a6) image.".

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (d) Section 11(b)(iii) of the Inferior Governments Act shall be created and shall state "(iii) The municipal police force of the District of Prominence shall have physical jurisdiction over all places marked in red in [this](https://gyazo.com/ac6f8906b04f909870428e2cc12bbc5e) image.".
