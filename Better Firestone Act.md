*Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the State of Firestone in Congress assembled,*

**SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE**

This bill may be known as the "Better Firestone Act".

**SEC. 2. EFFECTIVENESS**

This Act shall be effective upon completion of the constitutionally required process.

**SEC. 3. SEVERABILITY**

Should any part of this Act be struck down or declared unenforceable, then that part of the Act shall be considered severed from this Act, and the rest shall remain in full force.

**SEC. 4. ABOLITION OF MUNICIPALITIES**

The municipal governments of each municipality within the State of Firestone shall be abolished and the laws thereof repealed, and no government but Stapleton County and the State of Firestone shall exist over any such municipality.
