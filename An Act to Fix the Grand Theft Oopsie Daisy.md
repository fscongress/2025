*Be it resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the State of Firestone in Congress assembled,*

**SEC. 1. EFFECTIVENESS**

This Act shall be effective upon completion of the constitutionally required process.

**SEC. 2. SEVERABILITY**

Should any part of this Act be struck down or declared unenforceable, then that part of the Act shall be considered severed from this Act, and the rest shall remain in full force.

**SEC. 3. AMENDMENT**

(a) Chapter 3, Section 6(a) of the Firestone Criminal Code currently states: “(a) The act of grand theft auto shall be recognized as an unlawful criminal offense within the State of Firestone.”

(b) Chapter 3, Section 6(a) of the Firestone Criminal Code shall be amended to state: “(a) The act of Grand Theft shall be recognized as an unlawful criminal offense within the State of Firestone.”

(c) Chapter 3, Section 6 of the Firestone Criminal Code shall have its jail time penalty value amended to the range of 400-500 seconds in jail.
